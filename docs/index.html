Documentation

Overview

Track Command is a high-visibility digital dashboard designed for motorcycle racing and track days. It provides "at-a-glance" status updates so you can focus on wrenching while the clock does the work.

Schedule Setup

The app builds a full-day schedule automatically. Simply set your 1st Session Hour (e.g. 9:00 AM) and your Group Slot (e.g. :20 if you are the second group out).

Alert States

  • Meeting: A morning countdown to the mandatory riders briefing.
  • Warmup: Lead time to start engines and check tire warmers.
  • Gear Up: Final call to suit up and head to the pit wall.
  • Stand Prep: A blinking alert that triggers near the end of a session so you can prep stands and warmers in the hot-pit.

Custom Branding

Personalize the top-bar and labels in the Branding section. Control your Team Name, Racer Number, and the specific labels for up to two riders.

Split View

Enable the Split Screen toggle to track two riders simultaneously. Each rider receives independent color-coded alerts based on their specific session slot.

Debug Mode

Test your configurations by enabling the Time Override. This allows you to set a "fake" system time to verify alert transitions without waiting for the actual day to progress.

Persistence

All configurations are stored in your browser's LocalStorage. Your schedule and themes will persist through page refreshes and restarts.
